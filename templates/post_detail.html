The ferry left the dock at 6:40, the sky still soft with pink haze. I sketched the outlines of the skyline and wrote down three small promises for the day: keep the camera close, ask better questions, and let the pace stay slow.

By the time the city came into focus, I had a list of images to chase. The rest of the entry became a map of quiet observations: the sound of chains, the warm light on the deck, and the slow roll of the water.
